Interests

Barley enjoys chewing on sticks and bones but her favorite activity is chasing squirrels. She enjoys running through the woods and will tolerate swimming although it takes her a bit to get used to the water. Hiking/Camping are the perfect ways to allow Barley to enjoy her interests.

Personality

Barley is a self sufficient dog who needs no one, but she recognizes that others love her and will accept their love... in small amounts. She prefers "butt-scratches" over pets on the head or neck area. She has been accurately described as a Wolf-Cat as she is curious, independent, loves to explore/hunt, and is highly intelligent. While Barley is often seen doing her own thing at dog parks, she will occasionally play with dogs she has deemed worthy. Her playful side is a blast to witness! She loves to run as fast as she can and play "tag" with the other dogs.
